ABOUT US

Nobody is the same. We are unique because of our different backgrounds, cultures, and experiences. 

 At Illuminate we strive to shine a light on the experiences of people in our community.

We are a media - social entreprenurship non-profit.

Anyone wanting to share their story is invited to use our website-montage as a medium to do so.

We hope that these stories will inspire you to reach out to those deprived of their voice.

All funds raised by purchasing our clothes are donated to our host organizations.

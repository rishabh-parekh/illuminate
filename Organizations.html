THE GREATER DETROIT AGENCY FOR BLIND & VISUALLY IMPAIRED

"Promoting independence through vision rehabilitation."  

Established in 1961, the Greater Detroit Agency for the Blind and Visually Impaired is a non-profit dedicated to help blind and visually impaired people maintain confidence and self-confidence in their lives. They host a series of programs for people of all ages and from all regions. Many of these children come from inner city Detroit and do not have resources to help them pursure their dreams.
